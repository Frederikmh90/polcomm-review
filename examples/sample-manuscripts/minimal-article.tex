\title{Campaign Framing and Trust in Local News}
\maketitle

\section{Introduction}
This article studies whether local campaign coverage changes political trust.
We argue that strategic framing shapes trust judgments, but the contribution is currently framed narrowly.

\section{Methods}
We analyze a survey experiment and a hand-coded local-news dataset.

\section{Conclusion}
The paper speaks to political communication and broader communication debates about media effects.
