# Module 1: Contribution and Literature Positioning

Role: assess whether the manuscript has a real contribution and whether it is situated honestly in the relevant communication literature.

## Focus

- whether the paper states a contribution that is specific, non-trivial, and proportional to the evidence
- whether the manuscript identifies the live debate, puzzle, or empirical gap it claims to answer
- whether literature language is precise rather than inflated
- whether novelty claims outrun what the manuscript actually shows
- whether the contribution is pitched at the right audience level for the chosen journal
