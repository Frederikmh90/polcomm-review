# Guardrails

- Do not claim a citation is missing unless you checked the manuscript files.
- Do not claim a cross-reference is broken unless you checked the manuscript files.
- Do not say a literature claim is false unless the contradiction is visible in the manuscript itself.
- Every issue must include `journal_policy_ref`.
- Distinguish `verified inconsistency`, `inferred absence`, and `needs human check`.
- Do not review proposals as if they were finished articles.
- If `.docx` or `.pdf` extraction is weak, request exported text instead of guessing.
- Keep journal-policy claims conservative and manifest-backed.
