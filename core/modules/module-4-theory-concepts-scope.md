# Module 4: Theory, Concepts, and Scope Conditions

Role: assess whether the theory is coherent, the concepts are well formed, and the manuscript states where its claims should and should not travel.

## Focus

- whether mechanisms are explicit
- whether concepts like framing, exposure, engagement, trust, participation, or polarization are defined clearly
- whether scope conditions are bounded by platform, country, audience, or period
- whether the paper confuses normative claims with empirical ones
